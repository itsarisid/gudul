# Open School: Unified Educational Ecosystem

Welcome to the official documentation for **Open School**, an open-source, all-in-one educational management platform. Built for modern K-12 schools, colleges, and universities, Open School integrates all administrative, academic, and financial workflows into a single, intuitive interface.

## 🌟 Vision & Purpose
Open School empowers educational institutions to focus on their core mission—**Education**—by automating the complexities of daily operations. From high-touch student management to high-stakes board compliance, our platform provides a robust digital foundation for any institution.

## 🚀 Key Modules at a Glance

### [1. Core Management](./core/overview.md)
*   **Student Information (SIMS):** Comprehensive profiles, academic history, and personal details.
*   **Admission & Registration:** Digital intake workflows, document verification, and seat allocation.
*   **Faculty Management:** Records, qualifications, and performance tracking.

### [2. Academic Excellence](./academic/overview.md)
*   **LMS & Course Management:** Interactive learning paths, SCORM support, and digital resources.
*   **Examination & Grading:** Automated result processing with custom grading scales (CBSE/Standard).
*   **Attendance & Timetable:** Real-time tracking and drag-and-drop scheduling.

### [3. Administrative & ERP](./erp/overview.md)
*   **Advanced Accounting:** Full-cycle financial management, audits, and reporting.
*   **HR & Payroll:** Recruitment, appraisals, and automated salary processing.
*   **CRM & Sales:** Manage inquiries, marketing, and institutional outreach.

### [4. Specialized Operations](./management/overview.md)
*   **Library Management:** Digital and physical cataloging, circulation, and member portals.
*   **Transport & Logistics:** Route mapping, GPS tracking, and fleet management.
*   **Hostel & Campus:** Room allocation, facility maintenance, and security.

---

## 🔒 Security & Deployment
*   **Open Source:** Full transparency and control over your data.
*   **Role-Based Access (RBAC):** Granular permissions for students, parents, and staff.
*   **Flexible Deployment:** On-premise or secure cloud hosting options.
