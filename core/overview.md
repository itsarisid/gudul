# Core Management Modules

The Core modules of Open School form the backbone of the institution's digital infrastructure, ensuring that basic data about students, faculty, and admissions is always accurate and accessible.

## 👨‍🎓 Student Information Management (SIMS)
Manage the entire lifecycle of a student from inquiry to alumni status.

### Key Features:
*   **360° Profile:** Comprehensive view of academic performance, attendance, discipline, and medical records.
*   **Document Vault:** Securely store and manage scanned transcripts, birth certificates, and ID cards.
*   **Sibling Mapping:** Linked profiles for families, simplifying communication and fee management.
*   **Alumni Tracking:** Maintain connections with graduates for institutional networking.

## 🎟️ Admission & Registration
Automate the complex intake process with a digital, paperless workflow.

### Key Features:
*   **Online Inquiry Portal:** Capture leads from the school website and track through CRM.
*   **Automated Verification:** Rule-based checking of admission criteria and document completeness.
*   **Entrance Exam Management:** Schedule, conduct, and score admission tests within the system.
*   **Batch & Seat Allocation:** Automated student batching based on successful registrations.

## 👩‍🏫 Faculty & Staff Management
Empower your educators with tools to manage their careers and daily tasks.

### Key Features:
*   **Qualification Repository:** Track academic credentials and certifications for all staff.
*   **Workload Management:** Visual tools for classroom and non-classroom task allocation.
*   **Professional Development:** Record and track workshops, training, and achievements.
*   **Performance Dashboards:** KPIs for faculty engagement and student outcomes.
