# Academic Excellence & LMS

Open School provides a comprehensive environment for teaching and learning, supporting both traditional classroom models and modern, virtual learning environments.

## 📚 Learning Management System (LMS)
Deliver high-quality digital education through a integrated, user-friendly portal.

### Key Features:
*   **Rich Course Authoring:** Create and host videos, PDFs, and SCORM-compliant interactive content.
*   **Student Learning Paths:** Sequenced modules with prerequisite and completion criteria.
*   **Self-Service Enrollment:** Allow students to enroll in elective courses and extracurriculars.
*   **Forum-based Learning:** Integrated discussion boards for peer-to-peer and student-teacher interaction.

## 📝 Examination & Gradebook
Handle all assessment types with automated result processing and custom grading scales.

### Key Features:
*   **Online/Offline Exam Support:** Conduct secure digital tests or manage manual mark entry.
*   **Gradebook Configuration:** Highly flexible grading scales (CBSE-standard, Percentage, or Custom GPA).
*   **Automated Marksheets:** Instant generation of professional report cards and transcripts.
*   **Analysis & Trends:** Track individual and batch-level academic performance trends over time.

## 📓 Assignments & Homework
Streamline the distribution, tracking, and evaluation of daily academic tasks.

### Key Features:
*   **Digital Submission:** Students upload files or complete assignments directly within the portal.
*   **Evaluation & Feedback:** Annotation tools for teachers to provide direct feedback on submissions.
*   **Deadline Management:** Automated reminders for upcoming and overdue assignments.
*   **Plagiarism & Cheating Prevention:** Integrated tools for maintaining academic integrity.

## 💻 Live Classroom & Virtual Learning
Seamlessly bridge the gap between physical and digital education.

### Key Features:
*   **Video Integration:** Native support for BigBlueButton, Zoom, and Google Meet.
*   **Real-time Interaction:** Whiteboards, breakout rooms, and live chat within the session.
*   **Automated Attendance:** Track student participation automatically during live sessions.
*   **Recorded Lectures:** One-click archiving of live sessions for future student review.
