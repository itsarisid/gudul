# Academic Management

Open School provides a comprehensive suite for managing all academic activities, from curriculum planning to automated result generation.

## 📝 Examination & Evaluation

The platform is designed to handle the latest **CBSE Assessment Patterns** for both Scholastic and Co-Scholastic areas.

### Features:
*   **Automated Marksheets:** Generate CBSE-pattern report cards (8-point or 9-point grading).
*   **Weightage Configuration:** Define internal assessments (5/10/20 marks), periodic tests, and term-end marks.
*   **Result Consolidation:** Automatic calculation of final results based on defined weightage rules.
*   **Health & Physical Education (HPE):** Track student fitness levels for mandatory CBSE portal entry.

## 📚 Course & Batch Management

*   **Dynamic Timetable:** Drag-and-drop interface for scheduling classes and labs.
*   **Subject Mapping:** Link teachers and classrooms to specific batches and subjects.
*   **Student Batching:** Easily move or promote students across batches.

## ⏰ Attendance Tracking

*   **Real-time Alerts:** Automatically send SMS/app notifications to parents if a student is absent.
*   **Leave Management:** Allow students to apply for leaves and teachers to approve them digitally.
*   **Integration:** Supports Biometric, RFID, and manual attendance marking.
