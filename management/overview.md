# Specialized School Management

Open School extends its capabilities to specialized administrative areas, including transportation, library operations, and campus logistics.

## 📖 Library Management
A modern, digital library system designed for both physical collections and electronic resources.

### Key Features:
*   **Digital Cataloging:** Integrated ISBN/Barcode scanning for rapid book entry and lookup.
*   **Circulation Control:** Automated check-in/check-out with fine calculation and reminders.
*   **Student Book Portal:** Browse the library catalog, reserve books, and track reading history.
*   **Digital Library Support:** Host and distribute e-books, research papers, and audio-visual materials.

## 🚍 Transport & Logistics
Ensure student safety and operational efficiency with an integrated transportation module.

### Key Features:
*   **Route Mapping:** Geographic mapping of bus routes, stops, and student pickup/drop-off points.
*   **GPS Integration:** Live tracking of vehicles with app-based alerts for parents.
*   **Fee Integration:** Link transport fees to individual student accounts.
*   **Fleet Management:** Track vehicle maintenance, fuel logs, and driver documentation.

## 🏠 Hostel & Campus Management
Manage residential life and campus facilities with an intuitive allocation and maintenance tool.

### Key Features:
*   **Room Allocation:** Automated room and bed assignment based on student preferences and availability.
*   **Facility Maintenance:** Ticket-based system for reporting and tracking campus repairs.
*   **Meal & Mess Management:** Integrate with canteen services for automated meal tracking and billing.
*   **Security Logs:** Track student entry/exit and manage visitor logs for the hostel.

## 🍱 Canteen & Campus Services
Manage in-house dining and retail services with a cashless, integrated system.

### Key Features:
*   **Digital Wallets:** Students pay for canteen items via ID cards or the mobile app.
*   **Menu & Inventory:** Manage canteen menus, track stock levels, and automate vendor orders.
*   **Dietary Tracking:** Link meal choices to student health and medical profiles.
