# ERP & Financial Management

Open School incorporates powerful ERP-grade features to manage the institution's business operations, ensuring fiscal health and organizational efficiency.

## 💰 Fee & Finance Management
Automate the most sensitive area of school administration with precision and transparency.

### Key Features:
*   **Custom Fee Structures:** Define fees based on batch, category (EWS, General), or sibling status.
*   **Online Payment Gateway:** Securely accept payments via Credit/Debit cards, UPI, and Net Banking.
*   **Automated Invoicing:** Generate receipts and invoices instantly upon successful payment.
*   **Defaulter Management:** Automated SMS and app reminders for pending fees.

## 📊 Advanced Accounting
Full-cycle accounting system designed specifically for educational institutions.

### Key Features:
*   **Double-Entry Bookkeeping:** Maintain precise journals, ledgers, and trial balances.
*   **Institutional Auditing:** Clear audit trails for every financial transaction.
*   **Expense & Asset Management:** Track procurement, vendor payments, and physical asset depreciation.
*   **Multi-Branch Accounting:** Consolidate financials across multiple school branches.

## 👥 HR & Payroll
Manage your workforce with integrated recruitment, appraisal, and payroll tools.

### Key Features:
*   **Centralized Staff Records:** Biographical, academic, and professional staff details.
*   **Automated Payroll:** One-click salary processing with automated tax and benefit calculations.
*   **Appraisal System:** Structure-based performance reviews and feedback loops.
*   **Recruitment Portal:** Manage job postings, applicant tracking, and onboarding workflows.

## 📈 CRM & Institutional Marketing
Drive institutional growth with professional inquiry and marketing management.

### Key Features:
*   **Inquiry Pipeline:** Track prospective student inquiries from initial contact to enrollment.
*   **Email Marketing:** Automated newsletters, announcements, and promotional campaigns.
*   **Lead Scoring:** Prioritize high-value inquiries based on engagement and fit.
*   **Integrated CRM:** Unified view of interactions across all stakeholders.
