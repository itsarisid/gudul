# Compliance & Reporting

Open School simplifies mandatory school reporting and affiliation renewals, ensuring all data follows the latest **CBSE and Ministry of Education (MoE)** guidelines.

## 🏛️ CBSE Compliance (SARAS & OASIS)

The platform provides a dedicated module to manage **School Affiliation Re-engineered Automation System (SARAS)** requirements.

### Key Tools:
*   **OASIS Data Export:** One-click CSV/Excel downloads formatted for the CBSE OASIS portal.
*   **Mandatory Public Disclosure:** Automatically generates the "Public Disclosure" document for the school's website.
*   **Geo-Tagged Photos:** Mobile app integration for uploading labs/library infrastructure photos as required for inspections.
*   **SQAAF Self-Assessment:** Digital checklists and scorecards for the School Quality Assessment and Assurance Framework.

## 📊 UDISE+ (SDMS & Teacher Module)

Generate accurate reports for the Unified District Information System for Education Plus (UDISE+).

### Features:
*   **SDMS Automation:** Track Aadhaar seeding, category details (SC/ST/OBC), and CWSN (Children with Special Needs) status.
*   **Teacher Service Records:** Automated DCF Section 3 (Teacher Module) generation.
*   **Infrastructure DCF:** One-click generation of DCF sections (1 to 7) for direct entry or import.

## 📈 Analytics & Management Reports

*   **Principal's Dashboard:** High-level view of attendance, fee collection, and academic progress.
*   **Financial Reports:** Track fee deficits, scholarship disbursements, and overall school health.
*   **Library Reports:** Circulation statistics and inventory tracking.
